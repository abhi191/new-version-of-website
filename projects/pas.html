Presentation Analytics System



PAS (Presentation Analytics System) is an advanced learning experience powered by S/4 HANA and SAP Leonardo that accompanies and support individuals throughout their presentation journey/cycle. It give users a complete experience of delivering presentations and public speaking with confidence.

Using Artificial Intelligence, it gives precise analytics, relevant suggestions and feedback to the user to take action upon and also provides an immersive practicing environment using VR to make them confident before any such event or just to improve their skills.
