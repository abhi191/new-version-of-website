Background

Hello. I am Abhinav Gupta.
Currently working as a Product Designer at VMware.
Previously I was working with SAP Labs Bangalore to create unified experiences for Product & Innovation Tools team following a design first approach. Extensive collaboration with Design teams, Product Managers and Developers from global locations for user research, testing, design reviews and driving local and global projects forward is what I am doing on a day to day basis.

I have done my Masters in Information Design from National Institute of Design, Bangalore and previously done Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology from Indraprastha University, Delhi. I also have worked for 2.5 years as a Sr. UI Designer in Group10 Technologies, Gurgaon.

I have a keen interest in making simple to use, easy to understand and aesthetically appealing products which not only enhance the user experience but are also intuitive and innovative in nature.
